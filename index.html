ABOUT US

Join us for summer fun!

We’re thrilled to announce the return of the California Strawberry Festival at our NEW location, the Ventura County Fairgrounds. The festival heats up May 20-21, 2023 with all the same great fun you’ve come to love. Enjoy strawberry-inspired treats like build-your-own strawberry shortcake, chocolate-covered strawberries, strawberry beer, and more. Plus, over 40 food vendors, a Ninja Nation Obstacle Course, live music and entertainment, and over a hundred arts and crafts booths to check out. The exciting new beachside location provides iconic views, and Ventura offers up a vibrant downtown just a few blocks away, with hotels, restaurants, and nightlife. The Fairground’s convenient location right off the 101 freeway offers parking onsite, free shuttle service, and close proximity to the adjacent Amtrak station, making it easier than ever to get to the Festival.

Events & Arrtactions

The California Strawberry Festival is among the top festivals in the nation with over 40 food vendors, a Ninja Nation Obstacle Course, live music and entertainment, and over one-hundred arts & crafts booths! The California Strawberry Festival in Ventura is the perfect summer kickoff with proceeds benefitting the community in a BIG way. Join us in 2023 for an unforgettably sweet experience!

Art & Crafts

Approximately 200 artists and crafters from across the nation showcase their original works with a festive array of paintings, sculptures, ceramics, children’s toys, accessories, photography, textiles, outdoor furniture, jewelry, specialty foods, and more.

Eats & Treats

A variety of lip-smacking favorites include: strawberry nachos, strawberry pizza, strawberry funnel cake, strawberry chimichangas, chocolate dipped strawberries, deep-fried strawberries, berry kabobs, strawberry tamales, strawberry shortcake, strawberry beer, margaritas, smoothies, and even strawberry popcorn.

Entertainment

Want to get your groove on? Hot jamz. Cool tunes. Or just plain rockin’. We’ve got them all in a spectacular music lineup from some fantastic groups, including bands back by your demand! Kids can enjoy non-stop live performances throughout the day under the tented big top with puppet & magic shows.

The California Strawberry Festival is Back!

We’re thrilled to announce the return of the California Strawberry Festival at our NEW location, the Ventura County Fairgrounds. The festival heats up May 20-21, 2023 from 10:00am-6:30pm with all the same great fun you’ve come to love. Enjoy strawberry-inspired treats like build-your-own strawberry shortcake, chocolate-covered strawberries, strawberry beer, and more. Plus, over 40 food vendors, a Ninja Nation Obstacle Course, live music and entertainment, and over a hundred arts and crafts booths.
